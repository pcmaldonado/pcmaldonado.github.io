About Me

My name is Paula Maldonado and I am a French-Chilean with a passion for data science.

I have an economics background, but after two years of a PhD on theoretical economics, I decided to make a transition to data science.

Having taken courses in mathematics, statistics and econometrics, I focused my learning on doing projects to get more experience and to learn and/or improve my knowledge of Python, SQL and Tableau, as well as HTML and CSS.

This is my portfolio, where most of my projects are published. Go take a look at it !
