About Me

My name is Paula Maldonado and I am a French-Chilean with a passion for data science.

I have an economics background, and after two years of a PhD on theoretical economics (long-term population dynamics), I decided to make a transition to data science.

Having taken courses in mathematics, statistics and econometrics, I have focused my learning on doing projects to get more experience and to learn and/or improve my knowledge of Python, SQL and Tableau, as well as HTML and CSS.
